FPS Integration Tool

Under my company PierreWare Limited and the Grey Gear Systems trading name, I have solely created, published and am continually developing the FPS Integration Tool for Unity.

For more content and information visit:

Asset Store Page
GG Systems Website

The aim of this tool is to aid the development of First Person Shooter games by making such operations faster and easier. The tool supports this by dealing with the game mechanics and behaviours of the involved GameObjects and Prefabs which can be edited intuitively.

The features of the tool involve:

  • Programmed:
    • Weapon firing, reloading, switching, activation…
    • Player & NonPlayer movement
    • Universal health, damage & respawn systems
    • Complete adjustable UI including main, pause, success and fail menus
    • Death phase & management
    • Collectable item system
    • Use of Custom Inspectors & ScriptableObjects
  • Audio Visual:
    • Player weapon and NonPlayer animations
    • Basic models, Materials, Textures & Sprites
    • Audio
    • Lighting & baking
    • Editor icons

The code is purely written is C# but the complex functionality is contained within a hierarchy of scripts that do not inherit from MonoBehaviour to attempt better utilisation of object orientated techniques such as the SOLID programming principles as well as Abstraction and Reuse.

Operations needed to support the availability of the product were also included and performed by myself, which covers:

  • Management of the Company in general
  • Updating product documentation and user learning resources
  • Website development
